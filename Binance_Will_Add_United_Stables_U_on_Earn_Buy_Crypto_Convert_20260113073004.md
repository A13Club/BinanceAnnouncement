# Binance Will Add United Stables (U) on Earn, Buy Crypto & Convert

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that United Stables (U) will be added to Binance Simple Earn, "Buy Crypto" and Binance Convert at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

U Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2026-01-13 08:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy U with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay or buy and sell U with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of U being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading U against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of U being listed on Binance Spot.
