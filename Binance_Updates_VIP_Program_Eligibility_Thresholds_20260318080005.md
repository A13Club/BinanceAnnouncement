# Binance Updates VIP Program Eligibility Thresholds

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance VIP program is evolving to better recognize the broad base of high-value users contributing to the platform, enabling us to identify and support them earlier in their journey as they deepen engagement across our ecosystem. 

As part of this, Binance is updating key VIP program eligibility criteria across trading and capital allocation, while ensuring tier recognition remains tied to sustained and meaningful engagement.

VIP Program Key Changes 

1. Lower BNB Holding Requirements for VIP 1-3

We are lowering BNB holding thresholds and aligning them across VIP programs, making VIP level progression more accessible while maintaining program integrity.

Enhanced Referral Eligibility for the Futures Referral & Affiliate Program

Effective from 2026-03-19, and in alignment with the updated Futures VIP thresholds, users will now be eligible to receive Futures commissions generated by referrals from regular to VIP 2 users, an increase from the previous cap of VIP 1. 

Additionally, Futures trading volume generated by referrals within this expanded VIP range will be included in Affiliates’ Futures Evaluation performance metrics.

3. New VIP Criteria for Holders & Investors, with Expanded Eligible VIP Levels Up to VIP 9

Users who qualify for VIP through holding or investing activities (including Binance Earn) will now follow a new eligibility framework under the Holder Program. This gives VIP users greater flexibility to allocate assets across products while maintaining the highest VIP level they qualify for.

In addition, BNB holdings will now count toward both overall asset calculations and the BNB holding requirement. The updated Holder program will include assets across all Binance accounts, including Spot, Futures, Funding, Earn and Alpha.

For example, if your account holds 25 BNB (equivalent to 150,000 USD) along with other assets worth 100,000 USD, your total account asset holding will be considered as 250,000 USD and 25 BNB. This meets the VIP BNB holding requirement, making you eligible for the new Holder VIP 2 status.This update also expands eligible VIP levels for holders and investors. With this change, users can now qualify for VIP 5-9 based on asset holdings, creating a clearer pathway for long-term holders and investors to reach higher VIP levels.

4. Introducing the VIP Rising Star Program 

We’re introducing VIP Rising Star, created to recognize and support high-potential users on their journey towards Binance VIP.

Users with a daily or 30-day average net asset balance of 30,000 USD (including 5 BNB or more) will be eligible for this exclusive title, created for engaged, growing holders who have not yet unlocked full VIP benefits.

VIP Rising Stars will receive personalized VIP support, access to curated events, and exclusive opportunities designed to help them progress to Binance VIP faster, while being rewarded along the way.

How to Join the Binance VIP Program

There are several ways to qualify for Binance VIP, including trading, holding, and borrowing. You can achieve VIP status by meeting any of the minimum requirements shown. 

VIP levels are updated daily at 08:00 (UTC) based on the applicable requirements. If you qualify under multiple criteria, your VIP level will reflect the highest level you meet. 

Become a Binance VIP and get access to a range of VIP-only benefits, including lower fees, higher limits, priority support, advanced insights, VIP swag, event invitations and more.
