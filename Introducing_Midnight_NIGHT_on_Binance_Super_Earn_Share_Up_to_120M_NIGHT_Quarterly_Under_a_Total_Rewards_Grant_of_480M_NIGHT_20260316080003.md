Promotion A Reward Calculation:

Binance will take multiple daily snapshots of each user’s subscription amount and the total subscription pool of the pool at any point of time, and calculate the final reward distribution based on these snapshots:

(User's daily active subscription in a pool / Total subscription from all eligible users in that pool) * Total NIGHT rewards allocated to the pool per day

Notes:

Users may participate in multiple pools and their total rewards = Sum of rewards across all eligible pools. Each pool has its own reward allocation. For example, the same subscription value (in USDT equiv.) in the BTC pool vs. the ADA pool may accrue at different rates, depending on the total subscriptions within each respective pool.At maturity of user's positions, Binance will redeem the digital assets/digital currencies subscribed to Super Earn to Flexible Products at the end of the agreed subscription period.

Daily Glacier Airdrop reward is calculated as:
