PAX Gold (PAXG) is a digital asset issued by Paxos and is the largest OCC (U.S.) regulated gold-backed token. Each PAXG token is backed by one fine troy ounce (t oz) of a 400 oz London Good Delivery gold bar, securely stored in Brinks vaults. Holders of PAXG own the underlying physical gold, which is held in custody by Paxos Trust Company. Because each token represents a specific quantity of physical gold, PAXG’s value is directly tied to and tracks the market price of gold.
