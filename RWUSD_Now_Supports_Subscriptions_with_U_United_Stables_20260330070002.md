About RWUSD:

RWUSD is a principal-protected yield product that offers users flexible-term subscriptions with daily APR rewards. Designed to provide relatively stable returns, it aims to perform consistently in both rising and falling market conditions. The yield of RWUSD is supported by income generated from Binance’s ecosystem and other sources, including benchmarked against real-world assets such as tokenized US Treasury Bills.
