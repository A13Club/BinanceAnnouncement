# Binance Will Cease Support for Deposits and Withdrawals of Tokens on Selected Networks - 2025-12-12

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance will be ceasing support for deposits and withdrawals of the following tokens via the networks stipulated below from 2025-12-12 08:00 (UTC). After 2025-12-12 08:00 (UTC), any deposits sent via the following networks will not be credited and may lead to asset loss.

Tranchess (CHESS) via Ethereum NetworkdForce (DF) via BNB Smart ChainAavegotchi (GHST) via Polygon Network
