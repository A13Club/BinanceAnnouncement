# Binance Updates BNB Boost Interest Payment Method

This is a general Binance Exchange Notice. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians, 

Binance will implement an update to the interest payment method for the BNB Boost program. 

What’s Changing:

Effective 2026-03-01, the previous payment method via Binance Pay will be discontinued. 

All applicable interest (and any related fees or charges, as detailed in the BNB Boost terms and conditions) related to participation in the BNB Boost program will be:

Invoiced on the 1st of each month; and Automatically deducted from the master account’s Binance Spot Account on the 20th of each month:USDC for accounts in MiCA region;USDT for accounts in non MiCA region.

Next Steps:

No action is required. However, BNB Boost participants must ensure that they have a sufficient balance in their master account’s Spot Account before the 20th of each month to avoid any disruption. 

Please note that in the event of non-payment, partial payment or late payment of any interest (or any related fees or charges), we reserve the right to suspend or terminate access to the BNB Boost Program.
