# Binance Will Support ERC20 Integration of NIL (Nillion) and Discontinue Mainnet Support

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance will support the integration of NIL (Nillion) via the Ethereum network (ERC20) and stop supporting its mainnet.

General

Deposits and WithdrawalsAt 2026-03-20 03:00 (UTC), deposits and withdrawals of the mainnet NIL tokens will be suspended. Users should ensure they leave sufficient time for their mainnet NIL tokens deposits to be fully processed prior to this time. Binance will make a separate announcement after the event is completed to notify users when deposits and withdrawals of the ERC20 NIL tokens have opened.After the event is complete, deposits and withdrawals of the mainnet NIL tokens will no longer be supported.Spot trading, Margin trading, and Futures trading services will not be impacted by the discontinuation of NIL mainnet support.Binance will handle all technical requirements for users who are involved in this event.Ethereum network NIL token smart contract address:ERC20More Information, please refer to the project’s official announcement.
