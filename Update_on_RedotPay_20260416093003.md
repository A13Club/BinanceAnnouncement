# Update on RedotPay

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

As part of our ongoing review of our merchant partners, Binance Pay has discontinued support for Binance Pay features and functionality on the RedotPay platform, effective as of 2026-04-03.

Binance Pay continues to be available for users to send crypto to friends and family via email, phone number, or Binance ID (UID), as well as make crypto payments to over 21 million merchants worldwide, including national QR networks in multiple markets globally.

View some of our participating Binance Pay Merchants here. Binance Pay continues to explore new Pay Merchant partners and offer users more choices. Interested parties can contact us at https://merchant.%domain%/en.

We appreciate your understanding and continued support and we thank you for being a valued customer of Binance. 

Thank you for your support!
