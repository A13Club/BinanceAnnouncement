# Binance Will Add USD on Convert

This is a general announcement and marketing communication. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Users will be able to start trading USD against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees from 2025-11-18 15:00 (UTC), or within one hour of USD being listed on Binance Spot.

USD Deposit and Withdrawal

Binance now also supports USD deposits and withdrawals in select regions, powered by BPay Global. Payment methods include SWIFT bank transfer, credit/debit card, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Refer to this link for details on the limited-time promotion.
