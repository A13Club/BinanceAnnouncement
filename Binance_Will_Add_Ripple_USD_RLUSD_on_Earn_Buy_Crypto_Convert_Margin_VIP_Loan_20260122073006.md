# Binance Will Add Ripple USD (RLUSD) on Earn, Buy Crypto, Convert, Margin & VIP Loan

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that Ripple USD (RLUSD) will be added to Binance Simple Earn, "Buy Crypto", Binance Convert, Binance Margin and VIP Loan at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

RLUSD Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2026-01-22 08:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy RLUSD with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay or buy and sell RLUSD with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of RLUSD being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading RLUSD against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of RLUSD being listed on Binance Spot. 

Margin

Cross Margin & Isolated Margin

Binance Margin will add RLUSD as a new borrowable asset on Cross and Isolated Margin, as well as the RLUSD/USDT pair on Cross and Isolated Margin at 2026-01-23 10:00 (UTC).

Portfolio Margin

Binance Margin will add RLUSD as a new borrowable asset on Portfolio Margin, as well as the RLUSD/USDT pair on Portfolio Margin at 2026-01-23 10:00 (UTC).

Notes: 

Please note that newly listed tokens tend to be volatile; users are encouraged to adopt stringent risk management strategies when trading such tokens.

Please refer to Margin Data for a list of the most updated marginable assets and further information on specific limits, collateral ratio and rates.

VIP Loan

RLUSD will be listed as a borrowable coin on VIP Loan within one hour of RLUSD being listed on Binance Spot.
