# Introducing Live Futures on Binance Square: Share Trades, Build Trust, and Earn

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to introduce a new feature on Binance Square: Live Futures! This feature brings real-time Futures trading directly to the Binance Square community, giving users access to authentic, verified trades.

By enabling Live Futures, creators can showcase their skills transparently, helping users identify trusted traders and avoid scams. This strengthens content credibility, deepens community engagement, and builds confidence in following and copying trades.

Key Highlights:

Live Futures Trading Display & Sharing: Futures users can enable Live Futures to showcase their performance — including profit & loss (PnL), open positions, and more — directly on Binance Square. By enabling this feature, users can confidently showcase their trading performance to the community, making it easy to highlight trading achievements and reach a wider audience.Featured Top Traders: Top-performing traders will be highlighted and recommended on the Binance Square homepage and the Futures ‘Smart Money’ leaderboard, boosting their visibility and helping them attract more followers.Flexible Privacy Controls: Traders can choose to make their positions public or restrict access with subscription access codes, allowing only private groups to view their Live Futures activity.

Why Use Live Futures:

Trust and Credibility: For creators, verified live trades boost your reputation and help you stand out as a trustworthy trader. For readers, access to transparent, real-time trading data helps you confidently follow and copy genuine traders, reducing the risk of scams.Engagement and Loyalty: Use subscription access codes to share exclusive signals with private groups, strengthening relationships with loyal followers.Creator Earnings: Through the ‘Write to Earn’ program, creators can earn up to 50% commission on trades generated by their content, turning valuable insights into steady income.

How to Enable Live Futures:

Step 1: Go to your Binance Square profile settings.

Step 2: Toggle on Live Futures to display your real-time performance.Step 3: Choose your privacy settings. Make your trades public or restrict access with a subscription access code.Step 4: Save your settings and start sharing your live trading performance with the community!
