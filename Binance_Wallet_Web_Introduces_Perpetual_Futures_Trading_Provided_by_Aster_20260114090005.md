# Binance Wallet (Web) Introduces Perpetual Futures Trading, Provided by Aster

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Wallet is excited to announce a major upgrade to Binance Wallet (Web): the introduction of perpetual futures trading. Users can now trade perpetual contracts with leverage directly from their Keyless Wallet, through a seamless and powerful trading integration. This new feature is provided by Aster, a leading perpetuals platform, and is initially available on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC).

Binance Wallet users will participate in Aster’s points system when trading Aster perpetuals through the Binance Wallet (Web) interface. Additionally, Binance Wallet and Aster will offer exclusive campaigns available only to Binance Keyless Wallet users.The first of these exclusive campaigns launches today. More details can be found below.

Key Features:

Trade Perpetuals with Self-Custody: Enjoy full control of your assets with our keyless, self-custody wallet.Wide Range of Collateral: Deposit and trade with a variety of assets on the BSC network, including BNB, ASTER, USDT, USD1, ASBNB, LISUSD, WBETH, BTC, ETH, CAKE, LISTA, and USDF. Robust and Reliable Trading: Provided by Aster, the feature offers a stable and efficient trading environment with deep liquidity, fast execution, and low fees. 

Shared Points System: All perpetual trades executed via Binance Wallet (Web) will earn Aster points according to Aster’s points rules and count toward Aster’s trading competitions and reward events.

Unified Experience: Access perpetual trading directly within your familiar Binance Wallet (Web) interface, without needing to connect to third-party dApps manually.Stock Perpetuals: Aster also offers Stock Perpetuals, including trading pairs for blue-chip stocks like Apple (AAPLUSDT) and Nvidia (NVDAUSDT), as well as popular exchange-traded funds, such as the Invesco QQQ Fund (QQQUSDT). 

How to Get Started:

Begin your perpetual trading journey in just a few simple steps:

Log in to Binance Wallet (Web) and navigate to the new ‘Perpetuals’ tab.Click [Enable Account], then [Approve Trading], and follow the prompts in the Binance App to complete the required confirmations and signatures.Click [Transfer] under the [Account] page. Select the token and enter the transfer amount, then confirm the transfer to proceed.Once your balance is confirmed on-chain, you’re all set to trade!
