# Binance Upgrades USDⓈ-M Futures Liquidity Provider Program (2025-11-12)

This is a general announcement and marketing communication. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

As Binance conducts regular reviews of its programs to provide users with the most value and competitive services, Binance launches a limited-time promotion to the USDⓈ-Margined Futures Liquidity Provider Program effective from 2025-11-12 00:00 (UTC).

Key Highlights:

From 2025-11-13 09:00 (UTC), all qualified liquidity providers will receive an increased maker rebate of 0.05bps on BTCUSDT Perpetual Contract on a daily basis.Promotion Period: 2025-11-12 00:00 (UTC) till further notice

Promotion Fee Structure:

TierPrevious Maker Rebates for BTCUSDT Perpetual ContractPromotional Maker Rebates for BTCUSDT Perpetual Contract1-0.001%-0.0015%2-0.003%-0.0035%3-0.004%-0.0045%4-0.005%-0.0055%

Rebates Distribution:

Please note that the additional 0.05bps rebate for BTCUSDT Perpetual Contract will be distributed daily at 09:00 (UTC), based on the previous day’s BTCUSDT maker volume.

Maker fee rebates for the original USDⓈ-Margined Futures Liquidity Provider Program will continue to be paid immediately upon trade execution. 

Daily BTCUSDT Perpetual Contract Rebate = Total Daily BTCUSDT Maker Volume from the previous day (00:00 to 23:59 UTC) * 0.0005%.
