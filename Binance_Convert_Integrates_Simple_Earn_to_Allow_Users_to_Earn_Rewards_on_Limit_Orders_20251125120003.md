# Binance Convert Integrates Simple Earn to Allow Users to Earn Rewards on Limit Orders

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. Terms and conditions apply. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to enhance the Binance Convert experience with the integration of Simple Earn. This new feature allows users to earn passive income on funds reserved for their Convert Limit Orders. By opting in, usersâ€™ reserved assets will be automatically subscribed to Simple Earn Flexible Products, ensuring their capital is always working, even while waiting for trades to execute.

Key Highlights:

Capital Optimization: Generate passive income on funds that would otherwise be idle while reserved for a limit order. This reduces opportunity costs and maximizes your portfolio's efficiency.Seamless Integration: The system automatically handles the subscription to and redemption from Simple Earn Flexible Products. A streamlined asset deduction sequence prioritizes your Earn balance, ensuring a smooth process.User-Driven Flexibility: This is an opt-in feature, giving you full control. You can choose whether to earn rewards on your reserved funds for each limit order.Wide Token Coverage: The feature supports all token pairs on Convert that are also eligible for Simple Earn Flexible Products, offering broad opportunities to earn interest.

How to Access:

Users may access this function via both the Binance website and App.

Step 1: Navigate to [Trade] > [Convert].

Step 2: Select the [Limit] order type.Step 3: Enter your desired token pair, price, and amount.Step 4: Click [Preview] to confirm order.Step 5: On the confirmation page, enable the "Earn interest before your limit order executes" option.

Step 6: Confirm and place your limit order to start earning rewards on your reserved funds.
