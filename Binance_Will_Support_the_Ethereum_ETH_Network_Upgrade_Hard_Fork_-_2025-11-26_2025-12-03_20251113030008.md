# Binance Will Support the Ethereum (ETH) Network Upgrade & Hard Fork - 2025-11-26 & 2025-12-03

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance will support the Ethereum (ETH) network upgrade and hard fork to ensure the best user experience.

In preparation for the Ethereum (ETH) network upgrade and hard fork, Binance will perform wallet maintenance for Ethereum (ETH) at 2025-11-26 06:00 (UTC). To support the wallet maintenance, deposits and withdrawals on Ethereum (ETH) will be suspended starting from 2025-11-26 05:55 (UTC), and be resumed when the maintenance is complete. The maintenance will take about one hour.The Ethereum (ETH) network upgrade and hard fork will take place at 2025-12-03 21:50 (UTC). Binance will suspend the deposits and withdrawals of token(s) on the Ethereum (ETH) starting from approximately 2025-12-03 21:45 (UTC).

Please note:

The trading of token(s) on the aforementioned network will not be impacted.Binance will handle all technical requirements involved for all users.Deposits and withdrawals for token(s) on the aforementioned network will be reopened once the upgraded network is deemed to be stable. No further announcement will be posted.There may be discrepancies between this original content in English and any translated versions. Please refer to the original English version for the most accurate information, in case any discrepancies arise.
