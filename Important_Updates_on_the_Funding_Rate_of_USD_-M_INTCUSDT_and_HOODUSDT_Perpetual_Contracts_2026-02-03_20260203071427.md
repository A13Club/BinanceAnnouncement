There are two components to the funding rate: the interest rate and the premium. The funding rate formula:Funding Rate (F) = [Average Premium Index (P) + clamp (interest rate - Premium Index (P), 0.05%, -0.05%)] / (8 / N)The funding rate might not always be 0% and will be affected by the premium index, the only factor when the interest rate is 0%, Notes:
