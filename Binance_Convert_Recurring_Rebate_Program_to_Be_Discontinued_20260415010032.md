# Binance Convert Recurring Rebate Program to Be Discontinued

This is a general announcement and marketing communication. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

We would like to inform you that the Binance Convert Recurring Rebate Program will be decommissioned on 2026-04-30 23:59 (UTC).

Key Details:

Program End Date: 2026-04-30 23:59 (UTC) After this date, Convert Recurring Trades will no longer be eligible for rebate rewards under this program.Final Rebate Payout: Users who have accumulated eligible rebates in the month of April can expect their last rebate to be distributed before the end of May 2026.

Please note:

Your existing Convert Recurring plans will continue to execute as normal,  only the rebate incentive will cease.Any rebates earned up to and including 2026-04-30 23:59 (UTC) will still be honoured and paid out before the end of May 2026.No further rebates will be accrued for any Convert Recurring transaction after 2026-04-30, including those that are part of existing Convert Recurring plans.

No immediate action is required from you. Your recurring buy plans will remain active unless you choose to cancel or modify them.

Binance is continuously working to enhance your experience and develop better programs that deliver even greater value to our users. Stay tuned for upcoming updates and new initiatives!

For more details on the original program terms, please refer to the Binance Convert Recurring Rebate Program announcement.

If you have any questions, please reach out to our Customer Support team.
