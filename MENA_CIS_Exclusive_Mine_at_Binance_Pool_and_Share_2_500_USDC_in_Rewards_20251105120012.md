Reward Calculation:

The total average BTC hashrate increase on Binance Pool is calculated as the difference between the user’s BTC hashrate on 2025-11-05 and their average daily BTC hashrate over the entire Promotion Period.

For example, if the promotion lasts 30 days and a user adds 150 TH/s for 10 days, their average BTC hashrate increase will be calculated as: (150 TH/s * 10 days) / 30 days = 50 TH/s.
