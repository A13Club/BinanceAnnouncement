This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. USDT is not available to users in EEA regions.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Earn is excited to announce that RWUSD can now be used as a margin asset in USDⓈ-M Futures trading under both Multi-Asset Mode and Portfolio Margin Mode. This upgrade allows users to utilize their RWUSD holdings as the margin assets for Futures trading while continuing to earn up to 4% APR rewards.

How to Use RWUSD as Futures Margin

Subscribe USDT or USDC to RWUSD via Binance Earn.Transfer your RWUSD from your Spot account to your USDⓈ-M Futures account.On the Futures trading interface, activate Multi-Asset Mode or apply for the Portfolio Margin Program.Your RWUSD balance will now contribute to your margin assets, and you can start trading USDⓈ-M contracts.

About RWUSD:

RWUSD is a principal-protected yield product that offers users flexible-term subscriptions with daily APR rewards. The APR is determined at Binance’s discretion and aims to benchmark yields in RWAs such as tokenized US Treasury Bills. RWUSD is the record of your subscribed assets and any accrued rewards with Binance only. It is not a stablecoin, security, fund, tokenized RWA, or on-chain asset, and it does not represent any user right to any RWAs.
