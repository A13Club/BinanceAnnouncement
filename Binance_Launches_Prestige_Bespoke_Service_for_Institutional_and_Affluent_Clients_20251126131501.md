Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce the launch of Binance Prestige, a new bespoke service tailored to institutional and affluent clients entering the world of crypto.

What Is Binance Prestige:

Binance Prestige is a premium service designed to deliver institutional-grade crypto access through personalized relationship management, enhanced operational support, and exclusive client privileges.

With Binance Prestige, investors can trade, invest, and manage crypto portfolios with confidence, backed by the infrastructure of the world’s most trusted and regulated exchange.
