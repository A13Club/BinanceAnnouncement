# Binance Will Add Zama (ZAMA) on Earn, Buy Crypto, Convert, VIP Loan, Margin & Futures

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that Zama (ZAMA) will be added to Binance Earn, "Buy Crypto", Binance Convert, Binance Margin, VIP Loan and Binance Futures at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

ZAMA Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2026-02-02 13:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy ZAMA with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay or buy and sell ZAMA with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of ZAMA being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading ZAMA against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of ZAMA being listed on Binance Spot. 

VIP Loan

ZAMA will be listed as a borrowable coin on VIP Loan within one hour of ZAMA being listed on Binance Spot. 

Margin

Cross Margin & Isolated Margin

Binance Margin will add ZAMA as a new borrowable asset on Cross and Isolated Margin, as well as the ZAMA/USDT and ZAMA/USDC pairs on Cross and Isolated Margin at 2026-02-02 13:00 (UTC).

Portfolio Margin

Binance Margin will add ZAMA as a new borrowable asset on Portfolio Margin, as well as the ZAMA/USDT and ZAMA/USDC pairs on Portfolio Margin at 2026-02-02 13:00 (UTC).

Notes: 

Please note that newly listed tokens tend to be volatile; users are encouraged to adopt stringent risk management strategies when trading such tokens.

Please refer to Margin Data for a list of the most updated marginable assets and further information on specific limits, collateral ratio and rates.

Futures

Binance Futures will convert the USDⓈ-M ZAMA Perpetual Contract pre-market trading to standard USDⓈ-M ZAMA Perpetual Contract gradually at 2026-02-02 13:00 (UTC). The transition period may take up to 3 hours, subject to price volatility and availability of stable index price. Trading and existing positions of the ZAMAUSDT Perpetual Contract will not be affected during the transition period.
