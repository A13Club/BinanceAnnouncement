# Introducing Binance Chat: Connect, Transact, and Trade All in One Place

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to introduce Binance Chat, a new feature built directly into Binance. Binance Chat is an integrated social hub that seamlessly merges real-time social interaction with crypto transfer, creating a closed-loop ecosystem for communication and value exchange.

Key Features:

Frictionless One-Tap Entry:  Access the chatroom directly from the top of your homepage or Binance Square profile — one tap to start connecting.Real-Time Market Discussion: Exchange perspectives and strategy in real-time in a deeply integrated, social-first environment.Interactive Social Tools: Enhance engagement by sharing Trade Cards and Red Packets directly within your conversations.Instant Asset & Info Flow: Experience the speed of one-tap crypto transfers, allowing information and value to move simultaneously.

How to Get Started with Binance Chat:

Access: Tap the Chat icon on the top corner of the homepage or search for Chat in the search bar.Add Friends: Tap the [+] icon at the top right of the Chat homepage to add contacts via Chat ID, UID, or QR code.Join Groups: Visit a creator's profile on Binance Square and tap [Chatroom] to join.Interact: Inside a chat, tap the [+] button to share Red Packets or Trade Cards.

By integrating communication, trading, and community building, Binance Chat makes it easier to stay connected. It’s another practical step toward making crypto simpler and more accessible for everyone.
