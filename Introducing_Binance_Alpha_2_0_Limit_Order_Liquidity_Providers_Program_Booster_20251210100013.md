Fellow Binancians,

To improve users' trading experience and boost liquidity for Alpha trading pairs, Binance is proud to introduce the Binance Alpha 2.0 Limit Order Liquidity Providers Program Booster. This program offers a trading volume rebate of 0.0025% on the following tokens: Yooldo (ESPORTS), Fartcoin (FARTCOIN), Pieverse (PIEVERSE), and Rayls (RLS) on Alpha Limit Order only. We warmly invite all eligible users to participate and seize this exciting opportunity!

Promotion Period: 2025-12-12 00:00 (UTC) to 2025-12-25 23:59 (UTC)

Eligibility:

Users must be enrolled in the Binance Alpha 2.0 Limit Order Liquidity Provider Program.Users must achieve a total trading volume exceeding 100,000 USD in Alpha 2.0 Limit Orders from 2025-11-12 00:00 (UTC) to 2025-12-11 23:59 (UTC).

Reward Calculation Method:

During the Promotion Period, the eligible user’s reward is calculated as the total trading volume (USD equivalent) of ESPORTS, FARTCOIN, PIEVERSE, and RLS multiplied by 0.0025%.The calculated total trading volume excludes trades resulting from Liquidity Provider self-trading and trades where the counterparty is another Liquidity Provider.The one-time rewards will be delivered before 2025-12-31 14:00 (UTC) and will be distributed in USDC to the user's funding account.

How to Apply to Liquidity Providers Program:

If you have not yet enrolled in the Binance Alpha 2.0 Limit Order Liquidity Providers Program and would like to participate, you can apply by submitting proof of your trading volume through the application form or by contacting your sales or key account coverage team.
