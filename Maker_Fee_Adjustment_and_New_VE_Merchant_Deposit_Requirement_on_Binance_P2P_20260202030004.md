# Maker Fee Adjustment and New VE Merchant Deposit Requirement on Binance P2P

This is a general announcement and marketing communication. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

In our ongoing efforts to maintain a secure and efficient trading environment, Binance announces the following changes applicable to the BOB (Bolivia) and VES (Venezuela) fiat currency zones, effective by 2026-02-02:

1. Adjustment to Maker Fee:

The maker fee for transactions within the BOB and VES fiat zones will be revised to 0.25%. This adjustment is intended to foster market liquidity while ensuring the sustainability of the trading platform.

2. Implementation of New Deposit Requirement for VE Merchants:

All users with KYC from VE who wish to become new verified merchants operating in VE markets on Binance P2P will be required to maintain a minimum deposit balance of 800 USDT. This measure serves to enhance transactional security and uphold compliance standards.
