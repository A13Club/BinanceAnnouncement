# Binance Expands Institutional Loan Product Updates

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

We are pleased to announce a series of enhancements to Binance Institutional Loan, expanding borrowing capacity, broadening eligibility, and introducing fixed-rate term loan structures. 

1. Leverage Cap Increased to 5x

Institutional Loan now supports up to 5x leverage for all eligible risk units, both existing and newly onboarded clients. No re-onboarding is required; the updated limit applies automatically 

2. Updated LTV Thresholds

To provide greater borrowing flexibility, the following LTV parameters have been updated:

ParameterPreviousNewInitial LTV75%	80%Transfer-Out LTV (excluding spot collateral)75%83%Margin Call LTV85%	85% (unchanged)Liquidation LTV90%	90% (unchanged)Transfer-Out LTV = (Outstanding Loan Principal + Outstanding Loan Interest) / (Aggregated Net Collateral in Margin Account − Aggregated Required Maintenance Margin)

Please note: Margin Call and Liquidation LTV thresholds remain unchanged. These updates represent an expansion of borrowing capacity, not a reduction in liquidation protection.

The new thresholds take effect automatically for all risk units. Clients who have not drawn additional borrowings will not see any impact to their current LTV position.

3. Expanded Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for Institutional Loan have been broadened significantly, widening access to Institutional Loan.

4. Fixed-Rate Term Loans Now Available

Institutional Loan now supports fixed-rate term loan structures, offering greater cost predictability for borrowers.

Upon expiry, full repayment of principal is not required as the outstanding balance may be carried forward. The applicable interest rate will automatically transition to the prevailing variable rate as published on the Margin data page.

What Is Binance Institutional Loan & How to Get Started

Binance Institutional Loan is a credit line facility that allows institutional clients to aggregate collateral across up to ten sub-accounts to borrow USDC or USDT for Margin and Futures trading, with loan limits from $1M to $10M. KYB-verified clients at VIP 1 and above are eligible. To get started, contact your Binance VIP Account Manager or visit our FAQ for full details.
