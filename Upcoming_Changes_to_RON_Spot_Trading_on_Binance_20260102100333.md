# Upcoming Changes to RON Spot Trading on Binance

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance will discontinue all Romanian Leu (RON) services as per the timeline below:

Spot:

Binance Spot will delist the USDC/RON pair at 2026-01-09 08:00 (UTC). From this time onward, any remaining RON balances in users’ Binance accounts will no longer be usable for spot trading. All open spot orders of the aforementioned trading pair will be automatically closed when trading ceases.

Users may continue to buy cryptocurrencies with their RON balances via Buy Crypto and Convert. P2P services will continue to operate as usual without any changes.

Thank you for your support!
