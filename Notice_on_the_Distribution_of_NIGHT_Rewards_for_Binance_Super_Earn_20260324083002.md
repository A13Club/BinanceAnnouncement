# Notice on the Distribution of NIGHT Rewards for Binance Super Earn

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Following the recent launch of Midnight (NIGHT) on Binance Super Earn, Binance is pleased to announce the NIGHT Rewards Distribution details for all eligible Super Earn users. 

With the positive feedback from our community, Binance is working to bring more upgrades to Super Earn, stay tuned for more details. 

About NIGHT Rewards:

Total NIGHT Rewards: 120,000,000 NIGHTPromotion A Super Earn (Special Rewards): 108,000,000 NIGHTPromotion B Glacier Airdrop Special: 12,000,000 NIGHTReward Calculation: Similar to the snapshot methods mentioned at launch and on the FAQ, Binance will take multiple daily snapshots of each user’s subscription amount and the subscription pools at any point of time, and calculate the final reward distribution based on these snapshots: Promotion A: (User's daily active subscription in a pool / Total subscription from all eligible users in that pool) * Total NIGHT rewards allocated to the pool per dayPromotion B: (Glacier Airdrop User’s qualified active subscription (in equiv. USDT) / Total of all Glacier users' qualified subscriptions (in equiv. USDT)) * Daily NIGHT Promotion B allocationSnapshot Details: Snapshots are taken daily of users’ positions from 2026-03-16 to 2026-03-24 (a total of 9 days) to calculate NIGHT Rewards.Each snapshot records user holdings as of the previous day (e.g., a T+1 delay after any subscription or redemption) to ensure accuracy. Consequently, rewards begin accruing one day after subscription, and redemptions are reflected starting the day after they occurred.Promotion A: Each user's reward is calculated as a proportion of their holding relative to the total holdings within each respective pool.Promotion B: Each eligible Glacier Airdrop user's reward is calculated based on their total subscription across all pools relative to the total subscriptions of all eligible Glacier Airdrop users.No further snapshots or NIGHT Rewards will be accumulated after the distribution. After the distribution, all open Super Earn positions will be auto-subscribed to Simple Earn Flexible.

About NIGHT Airdrop Distribution:

Reward Distribution: Starting from 2026-03-25, Binance will begin distributing NIGHT rewards to eligible users' Spot Accounts. This process may take a couple of days and may vary depending on operational needs - please allow sufficient time for all distributions to be completed. No additional action is required from users.Promotions A and B rewards will be airdropped separately, users who are eligible for both will see two distinct entries in their Airdrop Distribution History.Users can confirm the receipt of the airdrop via [Assets] - [Overview] - [History] - [Distribution]. The status of these airdrops can also be viewed on Binance’s Airdrop page. Updates may also be shared on Binance's official social media channels.Please Note: Users restricted due to account, regulatory, or unforeseen reasons during the subscription period may have their NIGHT rewards temporarily withheld pending case review, which may take several days to weeks. Affected users should contact Customer Support. Binance will assess on a case-by-case basis, requiring user cooperation. If the case remains unresolved, restricted users may not receive NIGHT rewards. 

About Super Earn Positions:

Auto-Subscription to Simple Earn Flexible: Following the end of the Promotion Period, all remaining Super Earn positions will be automatically redeemed and subscribed into Simple Earn Flexible Products from 2026-03-26 onwards. Users will continue to earn Simple Earn Flexible APR on their assets.Users may manage their Simple Earn positions under [Earn] > [Simple Earn].Users may redeem their assets from Simple Earn Flexible at any time. Redeemed assets will be returned to users' Spot Accounts.For Users Who Do Not Wish to Auto-Subscribe: Users may redeem their Super Earn assets before 2026-03-26. Early redemptions will not result in a loss of accrued NIGHT rewards, which have been calculated based on historical snapshots. Users will receive the full balance of their locked assets in their Spot Accounts within 48 to 72 hours. 

About Binance Super Earn:

Binance Super Earn is a limited-time campaign that allows users to earn Special Rewards, in the form of Special APR or Airdrops, by subscribing selected tokens to designated Super Earn pools.
