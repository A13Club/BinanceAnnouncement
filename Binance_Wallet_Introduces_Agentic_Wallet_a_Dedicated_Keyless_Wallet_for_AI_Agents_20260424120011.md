# Binance Wallet Introduces Agentic Wallet, a Dedicated Keyless Wallet for AI Agents

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Wallet is excited to introduce Agentic Wallet, a dedicated Keyless Wallet in which users can authorise AI Agents with a secure environment to trade, transfer, and manage assets within parameters set by the users.

A separate Keyless Wallet created under a user’s Binance account, Agentic Wallet provides a dedicated balance, configurable permissions, and real-time monitoring for safe automation, while keeping your main Binance Wallet fully separate. For Binance Ai Pro users, Agentic Wallet Skills are built in by default, making it even easier to get started with AI-powered trading and asset management.

To celebrate the launch, Binance Wallet is also launching a limited-time gas sponsorship and zero service fee campaign.

About Agentic Wallets

Agentic Wallets are wallets purpose-built for AI Agents to securely trade, transfer, and manage assets on behalf of users. They provide a separate balance, configurable controls, and real-time visibility, allowing AI Agents to perform wallet operations safely within user-defined boundaries.

This architecture enables users and developers to delegate wallet operations to AI Agents in their dedicated Agentic Wallet while keeping the assets in their primary wallets separate.

Agentic Wallet supports all essential wallet operations, including balance checks, transfers, trading, and order management. It integrates seamlessly with CLI, MCP, Skills, and OpenAPI workflows, giving developers and power users full flexibility while keeping the experience simple for everyone else.
