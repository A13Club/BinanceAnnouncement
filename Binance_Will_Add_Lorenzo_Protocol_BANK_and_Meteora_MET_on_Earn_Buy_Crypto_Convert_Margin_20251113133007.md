# Binance Will Add Lorenzo Protocol (BANK) and Meteora (MET) on Earn, Buy Crypto, Convert & Margin

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that Lorenzo Protocol (BANK) and Meteora (MET) will be added to Binance Simple Earn, "Buy Crypto", Binance Convert and Binance Margin at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

BANK Flexible Products and MET Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2025-11-13 14:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy BANK and MET with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Revolut or buy and sell BANK and MET  with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of BANK and MET  being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading BANK and MET against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of BANK and MET being listed on Binance Spot. 

Margin

Binance Margin will add BANK and MET as a new borrowable asset on Cross and Isolated Margin, as well as the BANK/USDT, BANK/USDC, MET/USDT and MET/USDC pairs on Cross and Isolated Margin at 2025-11-13 14:00 (UTC).

Notes: 

Please note that newly listed tokens tend to be volatile; users are encouraged to adopt stringent risk management strategies when trading such tokens.

Please refer to Margin Data for a list of the most updated marginable assets and further information on specific limits, collateral ratio and rates.
