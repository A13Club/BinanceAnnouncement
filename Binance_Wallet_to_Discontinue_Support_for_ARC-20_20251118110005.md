# Binance Wallet to Discontinue Support for ARC-20

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians, 

Binance Wallet will discontinue support for the ARC-20 wallet effective from 2025-12-15 10:00 (UTC). After this date, users will no longer be able to view, send, or receive ARC-20 assets within Binance Wallet.

Please be assured that ARC-20 assets remain secure on the Bitcoin blockchain. This change only affects the display and management of these assets within Binance Wallet, usersâ€™ assets will not be lost or frozen.

How to Transfer Your ARC-20 Assets

Users are strongly advised to manage or transfer their ARC-20 assets before the discontinuation date. For detailed instructions on transferring ARC-20 assets, please refer to our guide on transferring ARC-20 assets to a wallet.After support is discontinued, users can continue to manage and trade their ARC-20 assets by importing their wallet into other platforms that support the ARC-20 protocol, such as the official Atomicals wallet.

Please Note:

This update does not affect other inscription protocols supported by Binance Wallet, such as BRC-20.
