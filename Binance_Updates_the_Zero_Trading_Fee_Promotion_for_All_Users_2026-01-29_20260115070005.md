This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

As Binance conducts regular reviews of its promotion offerings to provide users with the most value and competitive services, Binance will be updating its VIP Program on the  Zero Trading Fee Promotion for all users at 2026-01-29 00:00 (UTC).

Key Updates:

Starting from 2026-01-29 00:00 (UTC), standard taker fees will apply to all users when they trade selected FDUSD spot and margin trading pairs, including BTC/FDUSD, BNB/FDUSD, DOGE/FDUSD, ETH/FDUSD, LINK/FDUSD, SOL/FDUSD, and XRP/FDUSD. Zero Maker fee will remain as it is for the aforementioned pairs. The trading volume of the aforementioned spot and margin trading pairs will be counted toward all users’ Market Maker and VIP tier volume calculation, effective from 2026-01-29 00:00 (UTC). 

For large size trade requirements, users can use the OTC Desk by querying through Spot RFQ or Telegram.
