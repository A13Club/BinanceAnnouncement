# ChainOpera AI (COAI) Will Be Available on Binance Alpha and Binance Futures (2025-09-25)

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that ChainOpera AI (COAI) will open for trading on Binance Alpha starting 2025-09-25 07:00 (UTC). In addition, Binance Futures will launch COAIUSDT Perpetual Contract with up to 50x leverage at 2025-09-25 07:30 (UTC).

Binance is the first platform to feature and open trading for ChainOpera AI (COAI) on Binance Alpha and Binance Futures. In celebration of this launch, there is an exclusive token airdrop for eligible Binance users. Eligible users need to use Binance Alpha Points to claim their airdrop on the Binance Alpha Events Page between 2025-09-25 07:00 (UTC) and 2025-09-26 07:00 (UTC). Binance Alpha Events Page can be accessed via the Binance App using the search function, and more information on how to claim the Alpha airdrop using Binance Alpha Points can be viewed here.
