# Binance Futures Will Apply New ROI Method to Copy Trading and Update ROI for All Lead Portfolios

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Futures Copy Trading will update the ROI calculation method which will apply to all existing active lead portfolios, effective from 2025-11-12 03:00 (UTC). 

The new ROI calculation method introduces a new concept of “Maxbasebalance” to replace accumulated deposits, providing a more accurate reflection of a trader’s performance.

For more information on the new ROI calculation, please refer to “Portfolio Performance Indicators in Binance Futures Copy Trading”.

About Binance Futures Copy Trading

Futures Copy Trading allows users to copy experienced traders’ portfolios in real time. After determining the investment amount, the system will automatically copy trades from the lead traders’ portfolios that users select. Novice traders may familiarize themselves with the crypto market via Copy Trading, and build up their strategizing and trading techniques.
