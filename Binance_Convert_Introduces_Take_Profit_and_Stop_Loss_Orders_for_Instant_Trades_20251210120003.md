# Binance Convert Introduces Take Profit and Stop Loss Orders for Instant Trades

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. Terms and conditions apply. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Convert is excited to enhance your trading experience with the launch of Take Profit (TP) and Stop Loss (SL) orders for the Instant Order feature. This new functionality provides users with advanced risk management tools, allowing you to automatically secure profits and limit potential losses on your Convert trades.

Key Highlights:

Automated Risk Control: Seamlessly integrate Take Profit (TP) and Stop Loss (SL) orders with your Convert Instant Orders. Set predefined price triggers to manage trades efficiently without the need for constant market monitoring.Flexible and Dynamic Adjustments: Benefit from dynamic price validation against real-time market data. You can easily modify your TP/SL prices even after an order has been placed, giving you greater control over your trading strategy.Seamless Order Management: Create, edit, and cancel your TP/SL orders directly within the Binance Convert interface. Stay informed with real-time status updates and receive email notifications for any changes to your orders.

How to Access:

Step 1: Navigate to [Trade] - [Convert].

Step 2: Select [Instant] mode and choose the assets you wish to trade.Step 3: Activate advance settings for TP/SL.Step 4: Enter your desired Take Profit and/or Stop Loss prices in the fields provided.Step 5: Confirm the details and place your order.
