# Binance Will Add KGST (KGST) on Earn, Buy Crypto & Convert

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that KGST (KGST) will be added to Binance Simple Earn, "Buy Crypto", and Binance Convert at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

KGST Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2025-12-24 08:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy KGST with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Revolut or buy and sell KGST with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of KGST being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading KGST against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of KGST being listed on Binance Spot.
