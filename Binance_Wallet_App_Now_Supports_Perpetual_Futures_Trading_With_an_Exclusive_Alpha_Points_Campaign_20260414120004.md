Fellow Binancians,

Following the launch of Perpetual Futures on Binance Wallet (Web), Binance Wallet is excited to announce that perpetual futures are now available on the App version of Binance Wallet. This feature is provided by Aster, a leading perpetuals platform, and is initially available on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC).

Users can now trade perpetual contracts with leverage directly from their Keyless Wallet in the mobile app, bringing the same seamless and powerful on-chain trading experience from website to app. With this latest upgrade, users can access Binance Wallet Perpetuals anytime, anywhere through a secure and fully integrated mobile interface.

To celebrate the launch, Binance Wallet is introducing an exclusive Alpha Task Points campaign, available only to eligible Binance Keyless Wallet users.

Perpetual Futures Trading Key Features:

Trade Perpetuals with Self-Custody: Enjoy full control of your assets with our keyless, self-custody wallet.Wide Range of Collateral: Deposit and trade with a variety of assets on the BSC network, including BNB, ASTER, USDT, USD1, ASBNB, LISUSD, WBETH, BTC, ETH, CAKE, LISTA, and USDF. Robust and Reliable Trading: Provided by Aster, the feature offers a stable and efficient trading environment with deep liquidity, fast execution, and low fees. Unified Experience: Access perpetual trading directly within your familiar Binance Wallet (Web or App) interface, without needing to connect to third-party dApps manually.Multiple Perpetual Markets: Trade crypto pairs, blue-chip stocks, popular ETFs, and commodities.USD1 Denominated Market: Trade BTC, ETH, and SOL perpetual contracts in USD1-denominated markets, with lower fees and WLFI incentive eligibility.

Campaign Period: 2026-04-14 12:00 to 2026-04-28 12:00 (UTC)

Each UID is eligible for the reward once only.Rewards will be distributed before 2026-05-12 12:00:00 (UTC).Only trading volume completed through Binance Wallet (Keyless) on App or Web will count as valid.

How to Get Started:

Start trading Binance Wallet Perpetuals on App in just a few simple steps:

Update to the latest Binance App.Open Binance Wallet, and go to the [Trade] tab.Go to [Perps].Tap [Enable Trading] and follow the prompts to complete the required confirmations and signatures.There are two ways to deposit on the Perps page: Tap the [+] icon next to [Available] in the order panel.Scroll down to the [Balance] tab, then tap [Transfer]. Select the token and enter the amount to proceed.Once your balance is confirmed on-chain, you’re all set to trade.
