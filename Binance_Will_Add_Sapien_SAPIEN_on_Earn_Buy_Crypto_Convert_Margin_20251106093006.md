# Binance Will Add Sapien (SAPIEN) on Earn, Buy Crypto, Convert & Margin

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce that Sapien (SAPIEN) will be added to Binance Simple Earn, "Buy Crypto", Binance Convert, and Binance Margin at the respective dates and timings listed below.

Earn

SAPIEN Flexible Products will be listed on Binance Simple Earn at 2025-11-06 10:00 (UTC) and will be available for subscription.

Notes:

Sapien (SAPIEN) is the project on the Binance HODLer Airdrops program, which rewards BNB holders with token airdrops based on historical snapshots of their BNB Simple Earn asset or BNB On-Chain Yield balances. Users can subscribe to BNB Simple Earn Products (Flexible or Locked Products) or BNB On-Chain Yields to automatically enjoy HODLer Airdrops rewards.

Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can buy SAPIEN with VISA, MasterCard, Google Pay, Apple Pay or buy and sell SAPIEN with their account balances on the “Buy Crypto” page, available within one hour of SAPIEN being listed on Binance Spot. 

Convert

Users will be able to start trading SAPIEN against BTC, USDT, and any other tokens on Binance Convert at zero fees within one hour of SAPIEN being listed on Binance Spot. 

Margin

Binance Margin will add SAPIEN as a new borrowable asset on Cross and Isolated Margin, as well as the SAPIEN/USDT and SAPIEN/USDC pairs on Cross and Isolated Margin at 2025-11-06 10:00 (UTC).

Notes: 

Please note that newly listed tokens tend to be volatile; users are encouraged to adopt stringent risk management strategies when trading such tokens.

Please refer to Margin Data for a list of the most updated marginable assets and further information on specific limits, collateral ratio and rates.
