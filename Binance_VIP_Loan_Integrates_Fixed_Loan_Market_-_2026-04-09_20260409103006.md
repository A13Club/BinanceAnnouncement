Fellow Binancians,

Binance VIP Loan continues to provide a more seamless borrowing experience for our users. Key Updates:

Additional liquidity from Fixed Rate Loan: In addition to standard VIP Loan fixed rates, users can now borrow Fixed Rate Loan orders directly from the VIP Loan page.Streamlined Batch Borrowing: VIP Loan users may borrow multiple Fixed Rate Loan orders in one batch.Multi-Collateral Support: Users may pledge multiple asset types as collateral for a single loan, with a wider range of eligible collateral compared to standard Fixed Rate Loans.Flexible at Maturity:  When the term ends, users can auto-repay, repay early, or convert to a Flexible Rate loan to keep borrowing seamlessly.

How to Borrow a Fixed Rate VIP Loan:

Visit the VIP Loan page and create or select an Order Group.Select [Fixed Rate] and choose your desired loan asset from the [Fixed Rate Market].Browse available offers, select the ones you'd like to borrow, and confirm your selection.Review your selected orders and rates, then submit the loan request.
