# Binance Earn: Adjusting Multiple Dual Investment Trading Pairs

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. Terms and conditions apply. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance Earn will be making the following adjustments on Dual Investment trading pairs to enhance user experience:

From 2025-12-17 08:00 (UTC), the following trading pairs will no longer be supported: BTC/FDUSD, ETH/FDUSD, BNB/FDUSD, SOL/FDUSD, LTC/FDUSD, NEAR/FDUSD, ADA/FDUSD, AVAX/FDUSD, BCH/FDUSD, SUI/FDUSD, DOGE/FDUSD, BNB/BTC, BNB/ETH, ETH/BTC, and all trading pairs of TRUMP, POL, NOT, DOGS, and S.

From 2025-12-18 08:00 (UTC), Binance Earn will add Aster (ASTER), Ethena (ENA), Zcash (ZEC), Uniswap (UNI), and Aave (AAVE) on Dual Investment. Please see the table below for more information, particularly on the additional APR rewards.

Please Note:

Products with delisted trading pairs that have not yet expired will be automatically settled on their settlement date.Starting from 2025-12-17 08:00 (UTC), users will no longer be able to open new positions in Dual Investment for delisted trading pairs;Auto compounds related to delisted trading pairs will be automatically discontinued and will no longer continue after settlement at maturity.
