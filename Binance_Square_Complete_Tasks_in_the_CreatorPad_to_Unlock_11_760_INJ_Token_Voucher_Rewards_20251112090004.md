All verified Binance users can complete the following tasks to unlock a share of 11,760 INJ in token vouchers. 

During the Activity Period, eligible users who successfully complete Tasks 1, 2, 3, 4 and choose between Tasks 5, 6, or 7 will qualify for a share of the 11,760 INJ reward pool.

Task 4: Create content on X with the following criteria: A minimum of 100 characters about the project;Use the hashtag #Injective, $INJ and mention the project’s account @Injective;Content should be relevant to INJ and original to be eligible.Task 5: Trade a minimum of $10 equivalent in INJ in a single transaction on Binance Spot.
