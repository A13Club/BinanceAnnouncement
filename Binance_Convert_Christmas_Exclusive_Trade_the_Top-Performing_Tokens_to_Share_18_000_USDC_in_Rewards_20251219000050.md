Fellow Binancians, 

As part of the #MerryBinance Christmas Calendar this year, Binance Convert is excited to announce three new promotions to reward our community! During the Promotion Period, all users who confirm their participation and convert at least 15 USDC equivalent of any token to the top-performing tokens on Binance Convert, will stand to share a total of 18,000 USDC in token voucher rewards.

The top-performing token for each day (i.e., from 00:00 UTC to 23:59 UTC) refers to the token that undergoes the largest percentage increase in value from 00:00 (UTC) to 23:59 (UTC). Users are encouraged to review the Markets Overview and Trading Data pages before executing any trades.

Promotion Period: 2025-12-19 00:00 (UTC) to 2026-01-02 23:59 (UTC) 

Promotion A: Complete Trades on the Next Day’s Top-Performing Token to Equally Share 800 USDC in Token Vouchers Daily

Eligible users who fulfil the following criteria will be able to equally share a daily rewards pool of 800 USDC in token vouchers:

Confirm their participation by clicking [Join Activity] on the Promotion page;Make a single trade of at least 15 USDC equivalent of any token to Binance Convert’s top-performing token of the next day.

Promotion B: Complete More Trades on the Top-Performing Tokens at Binance Convert to Share 3,000 USDC in Token Vouchers

Only eligible participants of Promotion A can participate in Promotion B to receive additional rewards. Eligible participants will be ranked by the number of days they have successfully traded the top-performing token of the next day in Promotion A. 

The top 30* participants with the highest number of days of trading the next day’s top-performing token will then qualify for an equal share of Promotion B’s 3,000 USDC token voucher rewards pool.

Promotion C: Complete Your First Trade with Binance Convert to Share 3,000 USDC Token Vouchers

During the Promotion Period, the first 300 new-to-convert eligible users** who fulfil the following criteria will be able to qualify for an equal share of the 3,000 USDC token voucher rewards pool: 

Confirm their participation by clicking [Join Activity] on the Promotion page;Convert at least 30 USDC equivalent of any tokens on Binance Convert

Notes:

To qualify for rewards in Promotions A, B and C, users need to confirm their participation by clicking [Join Activity] on the activity page and complete the relevant trades on Binance Convert during the Promotion Period.Only Binance Convert trades with the Instant mode will count as successful trades toward Promotions A, B and C. The following types of trades will not count as successful trades toward Promotions A or B: Limit and recurring orders on Binance Convert;API trades on Binance Convert;Trade from stablecoin to stablecoin;Convert trades on old tokens that have previously undergone a token swap, redenomination or merging event, to the corresponding new token.*If more than 1 user is tied in the number of days trading the next day’s top-performing token, they will then be ranked by their total trading volume, where the user(s) with the higher trading volume will be qualified for the final 30 winners. **New-to-convert eligible users refers to users who have registered for a Binance account, completed KYC and did the first trade on Binance via Binance Convert during the Promotion Period.Alpha tokens are not eligible for this Promotion. For the purpose of this Promotion, tokens designated by Binance as Alpha tokens are excluded from: (i) selection as the “top-performing token” for any day; and (ii) counting as successful trades toward Promotions A, B and C. “Alpha tokens” are defined as the tokens identified by Binance as Alpha on the Binance Platform from time to time.
