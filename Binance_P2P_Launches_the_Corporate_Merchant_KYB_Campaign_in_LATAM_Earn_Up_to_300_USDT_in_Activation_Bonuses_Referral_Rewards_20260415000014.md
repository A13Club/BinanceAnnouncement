Fixed Activation Bonuses

Bonuses are cumulative, you can achieve multiple milestones to stack rewards. Bonus eligibility will be assessed after the Measurement Period from 2026-07-15 to 2026-10-15.

Milestone A: Verified Merchant (Basic)  —  30 USDT each

Achieve and maintain Verified Merchant status and meet the minimum average monthly maker volume for Bronze tier during the Measurement Period.

Milestone B: PRO Merchant  —  50 USDT each

Achieve and maintain PRO status and meet an average monthly maker volume of at least 100,000 USDT or 3 BTC during the Measurement Period.

Milestone C: BLOCK Merchant  —  100 USDT each

Achieve and maintain BLOCK status and meet an average monthly Block Zone maker volume of at least 100,000 USDT or 3 BTC during the Measurement Period.

Milestone D: Loyal Block Merchant  —  120 USDT

Maintain BLOCK status and meet an average monthly Block Zone maker volume of at least 100,000 USDT or 3 BTC for 12 consecutive months following the end of the Measurement Period. This is a one-time bonus, not a recurring annual reward. For this last milestone the Measurement Period will continue running until 15 July 2027 11:59 PM UTC.

Note: A merchant approved directly as PRO is considered to have achieved Verified Merchant status as part of PRO approval, but eligibility for Milestones A and B still depends on meeting the respective volume requirements independently. Fixed bonuses (Milestones A–D) are one-time rewards per Corporate KYB account and cannot be earned across multiple registrations.

Referral Fee Rewards

Eligible Corporate KYB merchants may earn fee-based rewards on referred merchants for up to 12 months from the referee's activation date.

Block Zone activity: 50% of net fees received by Binance from the referred merchant's Block Zone volumeNormal Zone activity: 20% of net fees received by Binance from the referred merchant's Normal Zone volumeOnly Corporate KYB merchants may act as referrers. Linked or related-party referrals are considered invalid.

Note: Fixed bonuses for Brazil participants are subject to a total pool cap of 5,000 USDT, distributed on a first-come, first-served basis. Referral fee rewards are not affected.
