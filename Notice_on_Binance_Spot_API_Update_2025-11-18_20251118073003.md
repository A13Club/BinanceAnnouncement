# Notice on Binance Spot API Update (2025-11-18)

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

As part of our ongoing efforts to optimize the Spot API, all Market Tickers Stream (!ticker@arr) has been deprecated. Instead, please use <symbol>@ticker or !miniTicker@arr as alternatives. 

The Market Tickers Stream will be removed from both the documentation and our systems at a future date. Further details will be provided in the Binance API Changelog.

Note: There may be discrepancies between this original content in English and any translated versions. Please refer to the original English version for the most accurate information, in case any discrepancies arise.

Thank you for your support!
