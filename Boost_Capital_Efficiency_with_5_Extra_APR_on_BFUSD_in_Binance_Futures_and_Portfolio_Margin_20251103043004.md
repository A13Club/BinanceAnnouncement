# Boost Capital Efficiency with 5% Extra APR on BFUSD in Binance Futures and Portfolio Margin

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region. Terms and conditions apply. 

Fellow Binancians,

Binance is excited to announce a promotion for users holding BFUSD in their USDⓈ-M Futures and Portfolio Margin Accounts. Eligible users who meet the daily Open Interest requirement will have the opportunity to earn boosted APR rewards on their BFUSD.

Promotion Period: 2025-11-03 04:30 (UTC) to 2025-11-17 23:59 (UTC)

How to Qualify:

Hold BFUSD in your Futures Account or Portfolio Margin Account.Maintain a minimum daily Open Interest of 1,000 USD across your USDⓈ-M and COIN-M Futures positions. Binance will take random daily snapshots of each user’s Open Interest and use the minimum recorded amount to determine their eligibility.

Reward Structure:

Qualified users will earn rewards based on their daily BFUSD balance, as detailed below:

BFUSD Balance TierApplicable APRFirst 1,000,000 BFUSDBase APR + 5% Boosted APRBalance exceeding 1,000,000 BFUSDBase APR

Note: The Boosted APR and the 1,000,000 BFUSD cap are subject to change during the Promotion Period. The latest Base APR can be viewed on the BFUSD product page. Please refer to the latest announcements for any updates.

Reward Calculation and Distribution:

Binance will take daily snapshots of users' BFUSD balances and Open Interest to determine eligibility for the Boosted APR.Base APR Rewards: The Base APR rewards on all BFUSD held in Futures or Portfolio Margin Accounts are calculated daily and distributed to users' Spot Accounts on a daily basis.Boosted APR Rewards: The additional Boosted APR rewards for qualified users will be calculated daily based on the eligible BFUSD balance (up to 1,000,000 BFUSD).The total accumulated Boosted APR rewards will be distributed in BFUSD to eligible users' Spot Accounts within 7 working days after the promotion concludes.

About BFUSD:

BFUSD is a reward-bearing asset redeemable for USD stablecoin. It offers rewards on qualifying balances and can be used as Margin in Binance Futures Accounts, allowing users to earn rewards simultaneously. By holding BFUSD, users can benefit from:

High Capital Utilization: Enjoy a collateral value ratio (CVR) of 99.9% when trading Futures under the Multi-Assets Mode.Stable Value: BFUSD is designed to maintain a stabler index price.Daily Rewards: Simply hold BFUSD to earn daily rewards.

Please note:

BFUSD is not a blockchain token and cannot be transferred outside Binance;Users can check their daily rewards under [Orders] > [Binance Earn History] > [BFUSD];Daily rewards are distributed before 08:00 (UTC) in the form of USD stablecoins, calculated based on users’ eligible BFUSD balance;
