# Binance Has Completed the ApeCoin (APE) Contract Swap on BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)

This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.

Fellow Binancians,

Binance has completed the ApeCoin (APE) contract swap on BNB Smart Chain (BEP20). Deposits and withdrawals for the new APE tokens are now open.

Please note:

The swap was conducted at a ratio of 1 old APE = 1 new APE. Users may obtain their assigned APE token deposit addresses here.Users can deposit old APE tokens (APEOLD), and swap these tokens for new APE tokens at 1:1 using the convert function. 

Withdrawals of old APE tokens will no longer be supported.
