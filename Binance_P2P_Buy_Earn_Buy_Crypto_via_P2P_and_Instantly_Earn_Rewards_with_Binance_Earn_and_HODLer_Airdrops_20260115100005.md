How to Access P2P Buy & Earn

Please ensure your Binance App is updated to the latest version (ver. 3.8.1 or above for iOS, ver. 3.8.4 or above for Android) to use this feature. 

Navigate to the P2P trading page via [Add Funds] > [P2P Trading]. You can use either the [Express] or [P2P] zone. Switch between zones by tapping the zone name at the top of the page.Select your desired fiat currency and the cryptocurrency you wish to purchase. You will see the respective Simple Earn APR for each token directly in the selection list.Choose a seller and tap [Buy]. After entering your purchase amount, enable the [Subscribe to Earn] toggle to automatically subscribe your purchased assets to Simple Earn. Please read the seller’s terms and conditions before placing an order.If this is your first time, you will be prompted to accept the Simple Earn terms and conditions.After confirming your order and completing the payment, your assets will be subscribed, and you can view them in your Earn Account. You may unsubscribe at any time
